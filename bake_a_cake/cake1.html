Featured Recipe: Chocolate Cake

Learn how to make a delicious chocolate cake from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the sugar and butter.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time.
  4. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add to the creamed mixture alternately with milk.
  6. Pour batter into a greased and floured baking pan.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
