Chocolate Cake

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup hot water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into greased cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Let cool and frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

The best chocolate chip cookies you'll ever taste!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cream the butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time and vanilla extract.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Soft, fluffy, and perfect for frosting with your favorite flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients, alternating with milk.
  5. Scoop batter into cupcake liners and bake for 15-18 minutes.
